Fostering plays a crucial role in the journey of animals on their way to finding permanent homes. It is a bridge between the uncertainty of homelessness and the security of a loving forever family. Foster care provides a safe and supportive environment for animals who may have experienced trauma, neglect, or simply found themselves without a home.
